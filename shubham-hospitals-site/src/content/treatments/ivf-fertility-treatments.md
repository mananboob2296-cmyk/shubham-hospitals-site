## Services

### IVF & ICSI (In-vitro Fertilization & Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)

In-vitro Fertilization (IVF) is a widely recognized fertility treatment where eggs are fertilized outside the body before being implanted in the uterus. At Shubham Hospitals, we follow the latest international protocols to ensure the highest success rates.

For couples facing male infertility issues, Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) is a powerful technique. It involves injecting a single sperm directly into an egg, which significantly improves the chances of fertilization and is used when sperm quality is a concern.

### IUI (Intrauterine Insemination)

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) is a less invasive fertility treatment where sperm is directly placed into the uterus around the time of ovulation. It is an ideal option for couples with mild infertility issues, such as those with unexplained infertility or low sperm count. IUI is a cost-effective and less invasive alternative to IVF.

### Fertility Preservation (Egg, Sperm, Embryo Freezing)

At Shubham Hospitals, we offer fertility preservation options, including egg, sperm, and embryo freezing. These services are ideal for individuals who want to delay starting a family due to medical reasons (such as cancer treatment) or personal reasons. Freezing eggs, sperm, or embryos can help preserve fertility for future use, offering peace of mind for those who are not yet ready to conceive.

### Male Infertility Evaluation & Treatment (Sperm Retrieval Techniques)

Male infertility is often a contributing factor to difficulty in conceiving. At Shubham Hospitals, we offer comprehensive male infertility evaluations, including tests to assess sperm count, motility, and morphology. Our expert team utilizes advanced sperm retrieval techniques, such as TESE (Testicular Sperm Extraction) and PESA (Percutaneous Epididymal Sperm Aspiration), to help men with low or absent sperm count.

### Donor Programs (Egg, Sperm, Embryo Donation)

For couples who are unable to use their own eggs or sperm, donor programs offer a viable solution. We provide egg, sperm, and embryo donation services, ensuring a highly confidential and compassionate process. All donors are thoroughly screened to ensure the highest quality and safety for your treatment.

### Recurrent Pregnancy Loss Evaluation & Management

For couples who have experienced recurrent pregnancy loss, our specialized team provides evaluation and management. We conduct thorough testing to identify underlying causes, such as genetic issues, hormonal imbalances, or structural problems with the uterus. Based on the results, we offer targeted treatments to increase the chances of a successful pregnancy.
