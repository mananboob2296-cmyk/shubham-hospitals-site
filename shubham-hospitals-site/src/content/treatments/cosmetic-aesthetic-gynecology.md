## Services

### Vaginal Rejuvenation (Laser & Non-Surgical Options)

Vaginal rejuvenation is a procedure designed to improve the tone, elasticity, and overall appearance of the vaginal area. At Shubham Intima Care Center, we offer both laser and non-surgical options to treat concerns like vaginal laxity (looseness) and dryness, commonly caused by childbirth, aging, or hormonal changes. These treatments are non-invasive, with little to no downtime, and aim to restore comfort, function, and aesthetics for a more youthful and confident you.

### Labiaplasty (Reshaping of Labia for Comfort & Aesthetics)

Labiaplasty is a surgical procedure that involves the reshaping or reduction of the labia (the inner and outer folds of the vulva) to improve both comfort and aesthetics. Many women opt for this procedure to address discomfort caused by excess tissue or for cosmetic reasons. Whether due to genetics, childbirth, or aging, labiaplasty can help improve the appearance and function of the intimate area, boosting confidence and overall well-being.

### Vaginal Tightening (Laser & Surgical Options)

Vaginal tightening helps restore the natural tightness and elasticity of the vaginal canal. This can be achieved through laser treatments or surgical options at Shubham Intima Care Center. Laser vaginal tightening is a non-invasive procedure that stimulates collagen production to enhance vaginal tone and elasticity, while surgical tightening provides a more permanent solution. Both options help improve sexual satisfaction, alleviate discomfort, and restore confidence in intimate relationships.

### Treatment for Vaginal Laxity & Dryness

As women age or undergo significant life changes such as childbirth or menopause, they may experience vaginal laxity (looseness) and dryness, leading to discomfort and reduced sexual satisfaction. Our treatments for vaginal laxity and dryness are designed to restore the natural balance of the vaginal area, improving both function and comfort. Laser therapies and hormonal treatments can effectively treat these concerns, helping women feel more comfortable and confident in their intimate lives.
