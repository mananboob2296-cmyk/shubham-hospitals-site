## Services

### Antenatal Care & Safe Delivery

At Shubham Hospitals, we understand that pregnancy is a life-changing experience. Our antenatal care is designed to monitor both the mother's and baby's health throughout pregnancy, ensuring a smooth and healthy delivery. Whether you're having a normal delivery or a cesarean section, we provide safe, expert care to ensure the best outcome for both mother and baby. Our team works closely with expectant mothers to prepare for delivery and ensure the entire process is as comfortable and safe as possible.

### Management of High-Risk Pregnancies

For women experiencing high-risk pregnancies, such as those with hypertension, diabetes, or carrying twins, Shubham Hospitals offers specialized care. We closely monitor these conditions to ensure that both mother and baby remain healthy throughout the pregnancy. With advanced diagnostic tools and expert obstetric care, we can effectively manage complications and reduce risks, helping women navigate high-risk pregnancies with confidence.

### Family Planning & Contraceptive Counseling

Shubham Hospitals provides family planning and contraceptive counseling to help women make informed decisions about their reproductive health. Whether you are looking to delay pregnancy, plan for a future family, or explore birth control options, our team offers personalized guidance and support. We discuss various methods, from hormonal contraception to intrauterine devices (IUDs), ensuring that you choose the method that best suits your health and lifestyle.

### Menstrual Disorder Management

Managing menstrual disorders is a key area of expertise at Shubham Hospitals. We treat conditions such as PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome), heavy bleeding, and irregular cycles, providing comprehensive care and treatment. Our team works closely with patients to identify the underlying causes of their menstrual issues and offer solutions that improve both health and quality of life. We tailor treatment plans to suit each woman's specific needs, from lifestyle adjustments to medical interventions.

### Menopause Management & Counseling

Menopause can be a challenging transition for many women. At Shubham Hospitals, we offer menopause management services that help women navigate this phase with ease and comfort. We provide counseling on managing symptoms such as hot flashes, mood swings, and sleep disturbances. Additionally, we offer advice on hormone replacement therapy (HRT) and other non-hormonal treatment options, ensuring that women have the support they need during this natural stage of life.

### Gynecological Problem Care

From fibroids and prolapse to infections and tumors, Shubham Hospitals provides comprehensive care for all gynecological problems. Our team of experts is equipped to diagnose and treat a wide range of gynecological conditions with advanced medical techniques and compassionate care. We offer both surgical and non-surgical options, ensuring that women receive the most appropriate treatment for their conditions.
