## Services

### Laparoscopic Appendicectomy

Laparoscopic appendicectomy is the minimally invasive surgical removal of the appendix, commonly done for appendicitis. Using small incisions, a camera (laparoscope) is inserted to remove the appendix, minimizing tissue damage and ensuring faster recovery. This technique offers less pain, smaller scars, and a quicker return to normal activities when compared to traditional open surgery.

### Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy (Gallbladder Removal)

A laparoscopic cholecystectomy is a minimally invasive surgery to remove the gallbladder, typically performed to treat gallstones or gallbladder disease. The procedure uses small incisions and a laparoscope to remove the gallbladder with greater precision and reduced recovery time. Most patients can return to normal activities within 1-2 weeks, compared to a longer recovery with traditional surgery.

### Hernia Repair (Laparoscopic & Open)

Hernias occur when an internal organ or tissue pushes through a weak spot in the abdominal wall. We offer both laparoscopic and open hernia repair options at Shubham Hospitals. Laparoscopic hernia repair uses smaller incisions and results in less pain, a shorter recovery period, and smaller scars compared to the traditional open procedure. We personalize the treatment plan to best suit each patient's specific condition.

### Surgery for Gastrointestinal Conditions (Colon, Rectum, Stomach)

We offer specialized surgery for various gastrointestinal conditions, including issues in the colon, rectum, and stomach. Our team is experienced in treating inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), colorectal cancer, gastric ulcers, and diverticulitis using advanced techniques. For many conditions, we prefer minimally invasive methods to reduce recovery times and improve outcomes.

### Piles, Fissure & Fistula Surgery

At Shubham Hospitals, we treat common ano-rectal conditions such as piles, fissures, and fistulas with both conservative treatments and surgical interventions. Using the latest techniques, including laser surgery and minimally invasive procedures, we offer effective solutions for these painful conditions, reducing recovery times and improving patient comfort.

### Andrology Services (Male Infertility & Urology-Related Care)

Our andrology services focus on male infertility and urology-related care, including the treatment of conditions like erectile dysfunction, varicocele, and male reproductive health issues. We offer diagnosis, medical treatment, and surgical options to help men address fertility issues and improve overall urological health.
