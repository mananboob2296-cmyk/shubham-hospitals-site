## Services

### Total Laparoscopic Hysterectomy (TLH)

TLH involves the removal of the uterus through small incisions. It offers quicker recovery times and minimal scarring compared to traditional open surgery.

### Myomectomy (Fibroid Removal)

A myomectomy is the removal of fibroids from the uterus while preserving fertility. The laparoscopic technique minimizes blood loss and ensures faster recovery.

### Endometriosis Surgery

This surgery treats endometrial tissue growing outside the uterus. Laparoscopic surgery provides less pain and quicker recovery, leading to better fertility outcomes.

### Ovarian Cyst Removal

Laparoscopic ovarian cyst removal allows for precise and safe extraction of cysts, with minimal risk and faster healing.

### Laparoscopic Ectopic Pregnancy Management

Ectopic pregnancy, where the fertilized egg implants outside the uterus, can be life-threatening. Laparoscopy offers a safe and minimally invasive way to manage this condition.

### Diagnostic & Operative Hysteroscopy

We use hysteroscopy to diagnose and treat uterine issues, such as polyp removal, septum resection, and adhesion removal, without the need for larger incisions.

## Benefits of Minimally Invasive Surgeries

- **Faster Recovery**: Most patients return to normal activities within **1-2 weeks**.
- **Smaller Scars**: Laparoscopic and hysteroscopic surgeries leave **tiny incisions**, reducing visible scarring.
- **Reduced Complications**: Less tissue disruption lowers the risk of infections and other complications.
- **Less Pain**: With smaller incisions and less tissue damage, patients experience significantly **less pain** after surgery.
- **Shorter Hospital Stays**: Many procedures are outpatient or require only **1-2 days** in the hospital.
