## Conditions we treat with keyhole surgery

Our [advanced laparoscopy & hysteroscopy](/treatments/advanced-laparoscopy-hysteroscopy/) services treat fibroids (myomectomy), ovarian cysts, endometriosis, ectopic pregnancy, and hysterectomy when required. Hysteroscopy treats polyps, uterine septum and certain causes of infertility with no external cuts. Our [general & GI surgery](/treatments/general-surgery-gastroenterology/) team performs laparoscopic gallbladder removal, hernia repair and appendicectomy.
