## Our fertility treatments

We offer the full range of assisted reproduction services, including IVF & ICSI, IUI, fertility preservation (egg, sperm and embryo freezing), male infertility evaluation with advanced sperm-retrieval techniques (TESE, PESA), donor programmes, and management of recurrent pregnancy loss. Every plan is tailored to your individual reports, never a one-size-fits-all approach. Your care is led by Dr. Manjushree Boob (MD, DNB, FICMCH, FICOG), Infertility & IVF Consultant, supported by a dedicated embryology team. For surgical fertility care, Dr. Manan Boob (MS ObGyn, Gold Medalist, DNB) provides advanced laparoscopic and hysteroscopic procedures.
