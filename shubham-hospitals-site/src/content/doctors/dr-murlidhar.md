Dr. Murlidhar Boob is a highly respected laparoscopic surgeon and surgical gastroenterologist with more than three decades of surgical experience. A pioneer of minimally invasive surgery in Amravati, he has performed over 10,000 laparoscopic appendicectomies, cholecystectomies, and GI procedures with exceptional success rates.

Trained internationally with a Fellowship in Laparoscopic Surgery (France) and Fellowship in Andrology (Germany), Dr. Boob brought cutting-edge techniques to patients in Central India at a time when laparoscopy was still new in India.

With his blend of surgical precision and compassionate care, Dr. Murlidhar continues to set the benchmark for advanced general and GI surgery in the region.
