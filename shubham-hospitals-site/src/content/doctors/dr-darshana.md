**Dr. Darshana Ajmera** is an accomplished obstetrician and gynecologist with specialized training in **ObGyn ultrasound and fetal medicine**. A distinction holder during her postgraduate studies, she brings a **modern, detail-oriented, and compassionate approach** to women's healthcare.

Her advanced training includes a **Fellowship in ObGyn Ultrasound (Mumbai)** and **Fellowship in Fetal Medicine (Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences, Kochi)**, equipping her with expertise in prenatal diagnosis and maternal-fetal care.

Dedicated to ensuring the **health and safety of both mother and baby**, Dr. Darshana is deeply appreciated by her patients for her **empathy, thoroughness, and gentle care**.
