A trailblazer in women's health, **Dr. Manjushree Boob** is among the first doctors to introduce **IVF and advanced fertility treatments to Amravati**. She is the co-founder of **Shubham Hospitals**, and over the last three decades, has helped thousands of couples fulfill their dream of parenthood.

With advanced training including a **Fellowship in Laparoscopic Surgery (France)** and **Fellowship in IVF (Germany)**, Dr. Boob combines international expertise with a deep understanding of local patient needs. She has successfully managed more than **1,000 IVF cycles** and overseen **20,000+ safe deliveries**.

Dr. Manjushree is known for her empathetic approach, guiding patients with sensitivity and clarity through every step of their fertility and pregnancy journey.
